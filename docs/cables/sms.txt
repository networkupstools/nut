COMPUTER        UPS
FEMEA DB9      MACHO DB9
2    ----------  6
3    ----------  9
4/8  ----------  5
5    ----------  8

- This cable is working with Manager III 1300 VA and 650 VA, SMS UPS's
- Jump in computer side pins 4/8 and connect to pin 1 in UPS side
- Use NUT blazer_ser driver
